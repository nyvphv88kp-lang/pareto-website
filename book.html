Frequently asked questions

You can reschedule via the link in your confirmation email up to 24 hours before the session. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.
All sessions are priced in USD. Stripe automatically handles currency conversion — you can pay with any major card regardless of your local currency.
Sessions can be recorded with your explicit consent. A copy would be shared with you via Google Drive after the session for review and revision purposes.
Group sessions require a minimum of 2 students and have a maximum of 4. This keeps the session focused and ensures every student has the opportunity to ask questions and receive meaningful feedback.
It helps to have your most recent marked work (tests, essays, or IA drafts) ready to share on screen. If you don't have anything, that's fine — we'll start with a diagnostic conversation to identify the best focus areas.
The Extended Essay is a high-stakes 4,000-word IB research paper with real university-level expectations. I offer specialist guidance at every stage: topic and research question selection, argument structure, source integration, criterion-by-criterion review, and draft feedback. The premium price reflects the depth and specialisation of this service.
