Our Approach

Teaching Philosophy

Economics is not a subject you memorise — it is a discipline you learn to think in. Having built a career in the private sector before entering education, I bring a practitioner's clarity to every session: real applications, precise analytical frameworks, and the examiner's instinct for what earns marks.

The gap between a 5 and a 7 is rarely talent. It is precision — command term mastery, diagram accuracy, structured evaluation, and well-chosen evidence. I teach that knowledge directly. Every session ends with something concrete that moves the grade. No filler. No vague revision plans.

The Pareto Faculty

Our sessions are delivered by curriculum-trained educators who combine academic rigour with real-world Economics experience. Every Pareto tutor is exam-literate and results-focused.

The Pareto Principle

80% of exam marks come from 20% of the right skills — diagram precision, command term awareness, structured evaluation, and well-chosen examples. Our sessions target that 20%, relentlessly.
